# Historical funding marks

These small local assets support the website's historical acknowledgement of CogNovo, the Marie Curie Actions and European Union FP7 grant agreement 604764.

- `cognovo-logo.png`: CogNovo programme mark; linked to <https://cognovo.eu/>.
- `marie-curie.svg`: Marie Curie Actions portrait mark; linked to the official Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions website.
- `eu-marie-curie-actions.jpg`: European Commission Research & Innovation / Marie Curie Actions lockup containing the EU emblem; linked to the official CORDIS record for grant 604764.

The marks are displayed unmodified and only to identify historical support. Their inclusion does not imply current funding or endorsement. All associated names and marks remain the property of their respective owners.
